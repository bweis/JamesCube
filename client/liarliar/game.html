How to play Liar Liar

Once prompted with a question, you must use your device to enter a bluff to fool your opponent into thinking it is true

The game consists of 3 rounds. Each round you get scored based on the following criteria


Each player you fool Finding the truth For selecting our lie
Round 1 +500 Points +1000 Points -250 Points
Round 2 +1000 Points +2000 Points -500 Points
Round 3 +1500 Points +3000 Points -750 Points
